About Us

ABM Academy – Excellent healthcare through continuous learning

Arlington British Medical Academy (ABMA) is an award-winning UK based medical academy which has diverse interests in medical education in the UK and across the globe. The company was established in 2013 “by doctors for doctors and healthcare professionals”. We offer:

  • UK Medical School Entry
  • Undergraduate Medical Education
  • Primary care CPD / GP CPD events
  • UK Royal College Exam Preparatory Courses
  • PLAB Courses / UK Licensing
  • COVID-19 related Training
  • Clinical Skill Courses
  • Our Premises & Facilities
